Me

I have a passion for javascript and modern web technologies. You can find me surfing the realms of wonder, testing the latest and greatest the web has to offer. I enjoy and pursue many sports, have a knack for adventure and nature in general, love music & movies, and while I haven't had the time for it as of late, get giddy when I can delve into a great computer game.

I maintain this website with pure scss / ng2. I believe that as advantageous as it is to design ui components with tried and true frameworks, much is to be learned from taking a walk on the wild side. I make sure to exercise that walk.
